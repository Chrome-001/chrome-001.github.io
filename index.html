⚡ ZFlash — Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 27, 2025

ZFlash ("we", "our", or "the app") is a flashcard and quiz study app. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use ZFlash.

1. Data We Collect

ZFlash stores all study data (subjects, lessons, quizzes, scores, and Zbolt wallet) locally on your device using an SQLite database. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information on our servers.

2. Advertising (Google AdMob)

ZFlash displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data to serve personalized advertisements. You can review Google's privacy practices at: https://policies.google.com/privacy.

Purchasing the Remove Ads option disables banner, interstitial, and rewarded interstitial ads. The voluntary "Watch Ad" rewarded ad remains available for earning Zbolt.

3. In-App Purchases

ZFlash offers in-app purchases (Zbolt packs, subscriptions, Remove Ads) processed entirely through Google Play Billing. We do not receive or store your payment information. Google's terms apply: Google Play Terms of Service.

4. Subscriptions

Monthly and Yearly subscriptions auto-renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the renewal date. Manage or cancel subscriptions in your Google Play account settings.

5. Children's Privacy

ZFlash is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the effective date above.
